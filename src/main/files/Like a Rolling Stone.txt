Title: Like a Rolling Stone
Description: "I wrote it. I didn't fail. It was straight," Bob Dylan said of his greatest song shortly after he recorded it in June 1965. There is no better description of "Like a Rolling Stone" — of its revolutionary design and execution — or of the young man, just turned 24, who created it.
Album: Highway 61 Revisited (Columbia)
Artist(s): Bob Dylan
Writer(s): Dylan
Producer(s): Tom Wilson 
Released: 1965
